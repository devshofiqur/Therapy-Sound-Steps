Illustration Direction Brief
✅ Style to use
  • Flat, soft vector illustration (SVG)
  • Rounded characters — warm and friendly
  • Palette: blues, peach skin tones, soft amber
  • Diverse representation (skin tones, hair types)
  • Scenes showing parent+child connection
  • Speech bubbles, sound waves as motifs
  • Minimal shading — clean cel-style fills
  • Reusable SVG components
❌ Style to avoid
  • Clinical or hospital imagery
  • Cartoon/game-like characters (too juvenile)
  • Stock photo realism
  • Dark or heavy colour fills
  • Complex scenes with many elements
  • Emoji-only decoration
  • 3D renders or photorealistic CGI
  • Sad or anxious-looking characters

Recommended sources: Undraw.co (customisable colours), Storyset by Freepik (animated SVGs), Open Peeps (character builder), or commission a custom SVG illustrator aligned with the brief above. All illustrations should be SVG for performance and scaling.
