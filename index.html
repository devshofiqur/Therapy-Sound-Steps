How It Works

Five simple steps.
One meaningful moment.

Each session is designed to feel like quality time, not homework. Here's what a typical practice looks like:

1

Listen to a sound

SoundSteps plays a clear audio example of the target sound. Your child hears it modelled correctly before they try.

2

Child repeats

Your child has a go. The app records their attempt gently, with no pressure or judgement — just a warm prompt to try.

3

You encourage

SoundSteps gives you simple, friendly prompts on what to say next. No experience needed — just your love and consistency.

4

Recording saved

Each session is quietly logged. You build a picture of your child's progress over time — something genuinely useful at future appointments.

5

Gentle progress suggestions

After each session, SoundSteps offers a small, actionable nudge for next time. Nothing overwhelming — just one kind step forward.
